
How do we represent the root of morphological complex words?

* flat

compounds
---------

- "normal" compounds where the rightmost morpheme is the head
(right-hand-head-rule).

each morpheme boundary is indicated by '_'

botervloot			boter_vloot
trainer/coach			trainer_coach
boter-vloot			boter_vloot
science fiction-schrijver	science_fiction_schrijver


- "left-headed" compounds

motie-Goudzwaard	motie_Goudzwaard
motie-Van Dam		motie_Van_Dam

particle verbs
--------------

verb_particle: particle is written after verb

opbel, opbelt, opbellen, opgebeld, opbelde, opbelden,
bel op, belt op, belde op belden op ===> bel_op
